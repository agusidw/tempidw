BEFORE IDEOWORKS

We didn't start as IDEOWORKS.

Before the name IDEOWORKS, there was Gopher Indonesia — part of an international digital advertising network. In 2011, Gopher Indonesia opened in Jakarta and became Indonesia's first, and only, Google AdWords Premier Partner for SMEs.

In 2014, IDEOWORKS invested in Gopher Indonesia — a move aligned with its own focus on e-commerce for SMEs. The goal set then was ambitious: serve 3,000 Indonesian SMEs within three years, through campaigns measured by real ROI, not vanity metrics.

That target didn't stay a target for long. It became who we are.
